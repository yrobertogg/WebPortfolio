About Us

Bountifulfoods is a local business founded by two parents in 2020 in Bountiful Utah. This marriage begins the foundation of this business due to the need for natural and organic food since her eldest daughter began to present allergic reactions to different foods and they wanted to find safe food options for her. Our mission is to offer the option of organic, healthy and affordable food for everyone. At the same time, we support local farmers, since we carefully select our suppliers, we give priority to those small entrepreneurs, checking that their products are grown in the most natural and clean way possible. In the end, we transform those fruits and vegetables into delicious smoothies that will strengthen you every day. Eating healthy never looked easier..
