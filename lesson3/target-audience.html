TARGET AUDIENCE:

The target audience is local business owners who want to be part of a great network of business owners who want to benefit from this red with contacts, events, education, legal advice, and state benefits. People who what to start a business and take advantage of the network, people who like to be educated on the creation of business too.

KAREN SMITH (PERSONA 1):

She is a 23-year-old girl, she is studying Industrial Management and she is almost finishing her degree, she likes the idea of starting a new cake shop. She planned to do some research on the local business to learn more about the cake shop field. She wants to find people that have started a cake shop before, to learn about the process, regulations, tax incentives, and some tips for her new business.
